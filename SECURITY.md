# Reporting Security Issues

The Wield team take security bugs in Wield seriously. We appreciate your efforts to responsibly disclose your findings, and will make every effort to acknowledge your contributions.

To report a security issue, please use the GitHub Security Advisory ["Report a Vulnerability"](https://github.com/wieldlabs/universe/security/advisories/new) tab.

The Wield team will send a response indicating the next steps in handling your report. After the initial reply to your report, our team will keep you informed of the progress towards a fix and full announcement, and may ask for additional information or guidance.
